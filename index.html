Our Story

Bringing Beautiful Flowers To Everyday Nigerians

Alexandra's Floral was founded on a simple belief
"flowers should not be a luxury reserved only for special occasions".

We started Alexandra's Floral to make thoughtfully designed floral arrangements more accessible, affordable and available to everyday Nigerians. Whether it's a birthday, a romantic gesture, a celebration, or simply a way to brighten someone's day, we believe flowers have the power to create meaningful moments.

Our Mission

To create beautiful floral experiences that help people celebrate, connect and express themselves through thoughtfully crafted flower arrangements.

Our Vision

To become Nigeria's most trusted floral brand, making fresh flowers an accessible part of everyday gifting and celebration across the country.
